How we started

KeeNok is a pop up focused on authentic dishes from Thailand as well as a few of our favorites from around the Asian continent. The idea sprang from our desperate search to find dishes from Thailand that just aren’t currently available in Chicago. We determined the only way we could enjoy these foods was to make them ourselves and so our journey began. Through trial, error and a whole lot of Thai cooking Youtube videos, we learned how to make some really tasty stuff. We started inviting friends over to try our latest creations and that eventually lead to the birth of KeeNok.
